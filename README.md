# Cible — AI On Your Finger

**Type:** Startup Concept / Business Plan  
**Date:** February 2026  
**Team:** UC San Diego MSBA Program

---

## Concept

**Cible** is a smart ring that puts AI assistance at your fingertips — literally. Unlike existing smart rings that prioritize hardware over design, Cible is **jewelry first**: beautiful enough to wear every day, powerful enough to answer your questions, start your tasks, and capture your day with a simple twist of your hand.

---

## The Problem

Three behaviors that current technology fails to fix:

| Problem | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Loss of Curiosity** | Friction of unlocking a phone kills spontaneous questions — most go unanswered |
| **Difficulty of Starting** | Knowing what to do ≠ doing it; the first step is the hardest |
| **Unreflective Living** | Without lightweight capture, days blur into each other |

---

## The Solution

A gesture-controlled AI interface embedded in a ring. Twist → ask. Tap → start a timer, a task, a reminder. The ring connects to your AI assistant without requiring you to pull out your phone.

**Key differentiator:** Most smart rings are ugly. Cible is designed to be worn, not just used.
